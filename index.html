Xi and Tei's night at the formal: A party from 7 to 9
A boy, Xi (Yushu Wu), successfully (unexpectedly) asked out his high school prom date, Tei (Elena Di Matteo). It was almost the hour, he couldn’t get his guts out, and speaking with Tei, pity, conflicted desire for sympathy, escapism, anxiety, and the dawning realization of adulthood all conjure up in him. He chooses to retreat to his chamber, and he is set to be confronted and punished for his immaturity. A study of spatiality, dollies, and art direction.

Yushu Wu
Born Shenzhen, China; 2007
First inspired by the poetic and the melodramatic, my films analyze emotions and the senses through rhythm, dolly/pan/zoom, and long takes. I’m interested in figures and mise-en-scène. I am a current student at Northwestern University studying to stretch observations into actions in documentary or fiction films, and through discourse, encourage truth-searching in a post-factual world.
